Livepeer AI, the latest innovation in the Livepeer ecosystem, introduces a **decentralized**, **open-source** platform for running **Generative AI** inference jobs on the Livepeer network. By leveraging the power of decentralized networks, Livepeer AI democratizes generative AI video capabilities, making them not only accessible but also affordable for developers and creators across the globe.

<Warning>The Livepeer AI Video Subnet is in Alpha, but we're eagerly anticipate the innovative applications our community will build with it!</Warning>
